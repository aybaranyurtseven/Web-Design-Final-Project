Concept Artist

At Full Snack Developers, we are looking for innovative teammates who will improve themselves every day and spread their positive energy around.
Seniority Team Type of Employment Location
Junior / Mid Mid Core Full Time Ankara Office
What Do We Expect?
  • Being enthusiastic about designing stylized art for the Mid-Core game.
  • Having a high level of knowledge about color, lighting and composition.
  • Contributing to the creative process of world-building.
  • Entrepreneurial mindset, high energy, motivation and a strong sense of ownership.
  • Ability to work as a great team player.
  • Ability to design organic and inorganic concept arts, based on Art Direction.
  • Ability to texture paint 3D assets, based on Art Direction.
  • Proficiency in tools like Photoshop and Substance Painter.
What You Should Expect?
  • As part of our team, you’ll be working closely with art, design and development teams.
  • You will be responsible for concept art designing of buildings, characters, props, etc.
  • You will be responsible for texture painting of 3D assets made by the team.
  • You will be involved in the development of a highly anticipated mobile Midcore game.
  • You will work hard, learn a lot and have a great time with the team.
What Do We Provide?
  • A competitive monthly income.
  • A full meal card for all your meal expenses, including weekends.
  • A comprehensive private health insurance package in addition to SSI.
  • Generous bonuses based on performance.
  • Fun company organizations.
  • A strong team and work culture.
  • An experience of taking part in one of the most established companies in the industry.
